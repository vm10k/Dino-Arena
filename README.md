## Overview
Dino Arena brings competitive stakes to the familiar infinite runner mechanics. Unlike the original single-player experience, you are not alone in the desert. You will compete against other players simultaneously on the same track, seeing their progress as "ghosts" while the speed increases.

## How to Play
1. **Enter the Arena**: Customize your username and select your preferred game mode to jump into the queue.
2. **Live Multiplayer**: Watch your opponents running in real-time as red ghosts. Everyone faces the exact same obstacles at the exact same time.
3. **Avoid Obstacles**: Jump over cacti and duck under Pterodactyls. The game speed increases the further you run.
4. **Win**: The last dinosaur running wins the lobby's virtual prize pool.

## Game Modes
* **1v1**: A head-to-head duel for survival.
* **4-Player FFA**: A four-player free-for-all match.
* **8-Player Battle Royale**: The ultimate test of endurance against seven other players.
* **Practice Mode**: A free-to-play mode to hone your skills without the stakes.

## Arena Rules
* **Real-Time Interaction**: Opponents appear as red ghosts. You cannot physically collide with them, but you can track their position relative to yours.
* **Sudden Death**: Hitting any obstacle results in immediate elimination from the round.
* **Winner Takes All**: Only the final survivor is awarded the prize pool.
