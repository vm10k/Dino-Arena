## Overview
Dino Arena brings competitive stakes to the familiar infinite runner mechanics. Unlike the original single-player experience, you are not alone in the desert. You will compete against other players simultaneously on the exact same track, seeing their progress as "ghosts" in real-time while the speed constantly increases. 

## Web3 & dApp Integration
Dino Arena is designed as a B2B mini-game that can be seamlessly embedded into any existing dApp to drive user retention and generate protocol revenue.
* **All EVM Chains Supported:** Fully compatible with Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and BNB Smart Chain.
* **USDC Wagering:** Players can stake crypto to enter lobbies. The last dinosaur running takes the entire prize pool.
* **Protocol Revenue:** A 10% performance fee on all wagered matches is automatically distributed between your protocol treasury and the game developers.
* **Custom UI & Branding:** The game UI can be fully customized to match your dApp's branding, color palette, and design system.

## Infrastructure & Self-Hosting
We exclusively support self-hosted architecture for enterprise and dApp partners. You retain 100% control over your hosting, player data, wallet connections, and matchmaking smart contracts without relying on third-party game servers.

**Minimum Server Specs required:**
* Any Linux OS
* 1 Core CPU
* 250MB RAM
* WebSockets Enabled

## How to Play
1. **Connect & Stake**: Connect your Web3 wallet, choose your network, and stake USDC (or bypass this by choosing the Free-to-Play practice mode).
2. **Enter the Arena**: Customize your username and select your preferred game mode to jump into the queue.
3. **Live Multiplayer**: Watch your opponents running in real-time as red ghosts. Everyone faces the exact same obstacles at the exact same time.
4. **Avoid Obstacles**: Jump over cacti and duck under Pterodactyls. The game speed increases the further you run.
5. **Win**: The last dinosaur running wins the lobby's virtual prize pool.

## Game Modes
* **Practice Mode (Free-to-Play)**: A completely free mode to enjoy the game, race against others, and hone your skills without any stakes or wallet connections required.
* **1v1 Duel**: A high-stakes head-to-head battle for survival.
* **4-Player FFA**: A four-player free-for-all match.
* **8-Player Battle Royale**: The ultimate test of endurance against seven other players for the maximum prize pool.

## Arena Rules
* **Real-Time Interaction**: Opponents appear as red ghosts. You cannot physically collide with them, but you can track their position relative to yours.
* **Sudden Death**: Hitting any obstacle results in immediate elimination from the round.
* **Winner Takes All**: Only the final survivor is awarded the prize pool (minus the 10% protocol/dev fee).
